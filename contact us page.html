Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of using Flex Machine Garage's home service for car maintenance and repair?
Using Flex Machine Garage's home service eliminates the need for customers to visit the physical garage location, saving them time and effort. Customers can schedule an appointment for a certified technician to come to their location, assess the vehicle's needs, and provide on-site service.
Can customers sell their used cars to Flex Machine Garage? If so, what is the process?
Yes, customers can sell their used cars to Flex Machine Garage. The process involves filling out an online form with information about the vehicle, including make, model, year, mileage, and condition. After reviewing the form, a representative from Flex Machine Garage will contact the customer to schedule an appraisal of the vehicle. The appraisal will determine the vehicle's value, and if the customer agrees with the offer, the sale can be completed.

Does Flex Machine Garage offer any warranties on their used cars for sale?

Yes, Flex Machine Garage offers warranties on their used cars for sale. The warranty duration and coverage depend on the specific vehicle and package selected by the customer.
